Inktober: Eyeball

Just kidding about that last comic where i said it was the end!!

I made this one real quick for Inktober 2022, and then I didn't do a single other comic for Inktober!

It was an idea I'd been kicking around for a while, though. I still have lots of little comics in notes that I want to make.

I just need to be in the right mood for it.

Transcript

An old man and a young man are standing somewhere.

Old Man: Why are your eyes closed? Such disrespect.

Both of their eyes are closed.

Young man: Because the Penance Spire cursed humanity to eternal blindness, you ancient dipshit.

Old Man: Typical millenial! Blame everyone but yourself!

Young Man: I'm a zoomer, boomer!

Old Man: Prove it!

The Young Man screams and fire shoots from his eyes. Heat radiates from his body as he powers up.

Young Man: So... This is "sight".

Smoke comes from the young man's eyes as he opens them. He then immediately closes them again.

Young Man: I prefer blindness.

Old Man: Such disrespect!
