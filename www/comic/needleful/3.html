TRANSCRIPT:

This comic is from the perspective of the Narrator.

Narrator: Why are so many people staring at me today?

Two people are currently staring at the Narrator in a public space.

Narrator: I need to check, or this'll bother me all day.

The Narrator enters the restroom and looks in the mirror. In the reflection is a spooky monster with a giant gaping mouth, and its tongue hanging out, drooling everywhere.

Narrator: Oh my god...

Narrator: When did this giant zit get here?
