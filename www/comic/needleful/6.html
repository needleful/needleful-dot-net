Nutrition

our daring protagonist is in a wild situation uh-oh!!

TRANSCRIPT:

A giant lady is holding Lee.

Lady: Oh ho ho! I am going to eat you!

Lee: I think you should reconsider.

Lady: I disagree!

The lady is holding a frying pan.

Lee: But why eat humans? We are small and smelly. Why not eat a more nutrient-dense food, like a big school of fish, or a herd of cows?

Lee is trying to wiggle free as he talks

Lady: Oh ho ho! I don't eat humans for nutrition.

The frying pan is on the stove now, and she's pouring cooking oil into it.

Lady: I eat them because it's funny! A lady, eating people? How silly!

Lee: That is terrible logic.

He's almost free. He's halfway out of her hand, flopping down.

Lady: In you go!

She throws him in the pan with a sizzle.
