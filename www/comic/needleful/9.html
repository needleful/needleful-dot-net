Morning Birds

once a crow kept following me everywhere and screeching at me. he was my friend
Transcript

Lee is looking out an open window.

Lee: I love the sound of birds in the morning.

A terrible nasty crow: SCRAW SCRAW SCRAW SCRAW

A bunch of crows are on a tree scrawing their bad noises. Crows are the worst bird and worst animal. A bird on the far end of the branch is skreeching.

We get a closeup. It has no beak, only a big mouth with teeth. It is still screeching.

The man is crouched down behind the closed window, staring out fearfully.
